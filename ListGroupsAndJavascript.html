The Legend of Zelda

The first game that was released in 1985. Link traveled across Hyrule to gather all 8 pieces of the broken Triforce of Courage to rescue Princess Zelda from the clutches of the evil beast Ganon.

The Adventure of Link

Princess Zelda had fallen into an enchanted sleep. Link again set forth across Hyrule to end her slumber. He encountered the darkness within himself and defeated it to awaken her.

A Link to the Past

The wizard Agahnim captured each of the seven maidens, descendants of the wise men that sealed away the beast Ganon. After weakening the seal with the final maiden, Princess Zelda, he was able to bring darkness upon the land and restore his master, Ganon, to power. Link fought back, traveling across Hyrule and between the realms of dark and light to rescue each maiden and stop Ganon again.
