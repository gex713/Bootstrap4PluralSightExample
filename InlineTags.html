Princess Zelda
The titular princess. She is strong in her own right, even if she gets captured a lot.
Link
Often confused as Zelda. He saves Zelda in almost every telling of the story.
Ganondorf
The human incarnation of the creature Ganon. He is smart and strong, often threatening Hyrule with everlasting darkness.
Great Fairy and the fairies of Hyrule
There are many fairies in Hyrule. Each bestows some kind of power upon link. The Great Fairy is the leader of them all.
